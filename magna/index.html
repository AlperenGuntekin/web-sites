Co-founders: Esen & Raymond Frangieh

It is often said that the apple does not fall far from the tree. For Esen Frangieh architecture was a way of life. Her father was a successful architect whom had completed many diverse projects. She grew up visiting construction sites, stopping by the office on the way home from school.
