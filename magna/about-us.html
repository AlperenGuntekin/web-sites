ABOUT MAGNA
Luxury, classic & timeless designs
over 17 years…
Raymond and Esen has worked alongside each other for over 17 years and founded Magna Insaat on 2014. They have carried out consultancy, architectural and interior design, fit-out and turnkey high end luxury residences and landscape designs in many regions including Qatar, Turkey, Africa and Lebanon. The company emphasizes to create personalized environments with luxurious and well refined details. Their designs focus on the classic and timeless designs where traditional and modern meet in harmony while creating intimate rooms as to larger spaces. Magna Insaat is specialized in manufacturing and delivery of all fit out works in great precision while collaborating with distinct Craft Masters from Turkey and Europe. The firm has always put the client’s requirements above all and aims to exceed all the expectations.
