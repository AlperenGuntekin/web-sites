BSS Bursa Süspansiyon Sistemleri was established in 2020 in the main industrial centre of the automotive sector in Bursa-Turkey and started to produce air suspension systems with the brand "Boldtech" for importand commercial vehicle and axle manufacturers. Starting with the principle of Sustainable Quality Products, BSS company is proud to offer its 30 years of experience and knowledge in the Air Suspension Systems sector to Turkey and the worldwide market. BSS Bursa Süspansiyon Sistemleri was established entirely with domestic capital and built on a total facility of 4.500 m2, 3.000 m2 of which is a closed production area.
